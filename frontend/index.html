How It Works

Four simple steps from complaint to resolution. Fast, secure, and completely transparent.

1

Submit

Register or choose anonymous mode. Provide incident details with complete confidentiality.

2

Review

Admin team verifies your complaint and assigns to appropriate investigating authority.

3

Investigate

Trained professionals investigate thoroughly, collect evidence, and take necessary actions.

Resolve

Case resolved with full transparency. Complete report generated and made available to you.
