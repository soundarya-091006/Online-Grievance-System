How to Submit a Complaint

1. Click Submit Complaint.
2. Fill required details.
3. Select category & priority.
4. Submit and track from Dashboard.

Complaint Status Meaning

NEW: Received and awaiting review.

IN PROGRESS: Investigation ongoing.

RESOLVED: Issue handled successfully.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does resolution take?
3–7 working days.

Can I edit my complaint?
No, once submitted.

Is my complaint confidential?
Yes, fully secure.
