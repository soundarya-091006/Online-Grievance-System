Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2024

Your Privacy is Our Priority: At SafeReport, we are committed to protecting your privacy and maintaining the confidentiality of all information you share with us.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us when using SafeReport:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number (optional)
  • Complaint Details: Information about incidents you report
  • Evidence: Files, documents, images, or videos you upload
  • Communication Data: Messages between you and our support team
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information

2. Anonymous Reporting

When you choose to submit a complaint anonymously:

  • No personal identifying information is collected
  • You receive a unique complaint ID to track your case
  • Your IP address is encrypted and not stored
  • Anonymous complaints receive the same priority as identified complaints

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Processing and investigating complaints
  • Communicating with you about your case
  • Improving our services and user experience
  • Generating anonymized statistics and reports
  • Complying with legal obligations

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • End-to-end encryption for all sensitive data
  • Secure servers with regular security audits
  • Access controls limiting data access to authorized personnel
  • Regular backups with encrypted storage
  • Secure file upload and storage systems

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information except:

  • With investigating authorities assigned to your case
  • When required by law or legal process
  • With your explicit consent
  • To protect the rights and safety of others

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing
  • Export your data in a portable format

7. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Authenticate your account
  • Analyze site usage and improve performance
  • Provide secure access to your account

8. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as:

  • Your account is active
  • Required to provide services
  • Necessary for legal compliance
  • Required for complaint resolution and archival purposes

9. Children's Privacy

SafeReport is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from minors. If we learn that we have collected information from a minor, we will promptly delete it.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
